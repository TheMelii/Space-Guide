Galxies

A galaxy is a huge collection of gas, dust, and billions of stars and their solar systems, all held together by gravity.

There are more than 100 billion galaxies within our universe. Those galaxies can be one of the three kinds of galaxies: elliptical, spiral or irregular.

The most common galaxy form, as well as galaxy form in which we live in, is spiral.
